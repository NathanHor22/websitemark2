Who we are

Syntech Solutions delivers cost-effective, sustainable VR solutions that transform education. By combining VR with haptic glove technology, we create immersive, hands-on training, especially for medical students practicing skills like suturing in safe, engaging digital environments.

What we do

Syntech Solutions is currently developing Synthmed, a VR platform built for medical career fairs that transforms them into hands-on recruitment labs. Using affordable ESP32-powered haptic gloves, Synthmed delivers realistic, 5–12 minute simulations with instant feedback and micro-credentials for students, while providing recruiters with real-time performance insights. This scalable, gamified approach redefines medical recruitment by shifting from resumes to proof of skill.
